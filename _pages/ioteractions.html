Ioteractions

About the project

Ioteractions is an Internet of Things company that provides an IoT communication platform for smart devices. They needed a logo and identity that represented connectedness, and felt fresh and modern.

The Ioteractions hexaon.

Since the startup was working on a number of different projects at the time, they also did not want to be boxed into a category and have to change their logo. The abstract hexagon allows for flexibility, and symbolifies connectedness.

The color scheme is modern and fresh, and allows the branding to distinctly stand out.
